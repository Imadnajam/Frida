# **FRIDA - High-Quality Media Resizing**

**FRIDA** is a SaaS platform that enables users to resize and optimize their images and videos without losing quality. Built with efficiency and performance in mind, FRIDA helps businesses, content creators, and individuals manage their media with ease, ensuring that all resized media maintains the original quality.

## 🚀 **Features**

- **High-Quality Image Resizing:** Upload your images and resize them to custom dimensions while maintaining the original quality and aspect ratio.
- **Video Resizing & Compression:** Resize videos to meet various platform specifications (web, mobile, etc.) while preserving quality. Compression is applied without visible loss.
- **Format Conversion:** Convert images to popular formats like JPEG, PNG, and WebP, and videos to formats like MP4 and WebM.
- **Cloud Storage:** Store processed media securely in the cloud with easy access and downloads.
